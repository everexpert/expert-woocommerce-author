Everexpert Authors for WooCommerce allows you to show product authors in your WooCommerce based store

== Description ==

Everexpert WooCommerce Authors is a perfect tool to organize your site, highlight the authors you have, and also helps as a filter for your customers at UX exploration. EWA extendes the product's description and presentation at your e-commerce site.

== PRESENTATION ==

[About Us](https://everexpert.com/) | [Community](https://www.facebook.com/groups/everexpert/) | [Documentation](https://everexpert.com/documentation/everexpert-woocommerce-authors/)

Whether we like to admit it or not, we are all influenced by authors. Authors are a guarantee for quality, they assure product recognition in customers.
Is essential to work with product authors for increase sales and generate reliability on your e-commerce site.
With this extension you can add product authors to your WooCommerce site.

= Requirements =
> * PHP 5.6 or higher (PHP7 recommended)
> * WordPress 4.7 or higher
> * WooCommerce 3.1.0 or higher

= Features =
> * Very easy to use, 100% free, no ads, no premium version exists
> * Assign authors to products
> * Associate a banner and a link to each author
> * Translation-ready
> * Visual Composer support
> * Minimalist design and fully responsive
> * Very lightweight
> * Shortcode: Display all authors
> * Shortcode: Display authors carousel
> * Shortcode: Display product carousel by author
> * Shortcode: Display authors for a specific product
> * Shortcode: A-Z Listing
> * Widget: Display authors as dropdown
> * Widget: Display authors as list (author names or author logos)
> * Widget: Filter products by author
> * Customizable authors slug
> * Show the authors in products loop
> * Import authors (migrate) from other authors plugins
> * Dummy data installer (logos by heroturko)
> * WooCommerce REST API support
> * WooCommerce built-in product importer/exporter support
> * Author tab for single product page
> * Favorite authors
> * Authors json import/export
> * Author structured data
> * And much more!


== Installation ==
1. Upload the plugin to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Is Everexpert WooCommerce Authors free? =
Yes, of course. This plugin is 100% free. No ads, no premium version exists.

= Where are the plugin settings? =
Go to `WooCommerce/Settings/` and click on `Authors` tab

= 404 error on author pages =
Go to `Settings/Permalinks` and click on `Save Changes` button to flush the permalinks
